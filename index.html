Why Choose Habitual Mini?

Traditional sanitary systems require large amounts of water and can easily fail in crisis situations. Our Mini module offers a simple, natural alternative.

  • Fast deployment – flat-packed and easy to assemble
  • Odorless & hygienic – absorbent materials and low humidity
  • Off-grid – no plumbing or electricity required
  • Eco-friendly – turns waste into compost

Mini Module

The Mini module is a lightweight, flat-packed dry toilet designed for disaster relief, humanitarian missions and off-grid camps. Each unit operates independently of any water or sewer network and uses an efficient composting system.

  • Fast deployment: assembles in minutes; no heavy machinery or infrastructure needed.
  • Odorless & hygienic: low humidity and absorbent material prevent germs and smells.
  • Off-grid: works without plumbing or electricity; optional solar power modules.
  • Eco-friendly: waste is converted to compost for parks and gardens.
  • Compact & modular: ships flat and can be expanded with accessories.

Our Story

Habitual began in 2021 with a simple idea: provide a sustainable solution to the global sanitation crisis. Our founders created the first prototype in 2022 and successfully sold it as a fully functional unit. By 2024, more than ten units were in use. In 2025, Habitual embarked on a new mission: helping humanitarian NGOs and governments deliver safe sanitation in disaster zones, refugee camps and remote communities.
