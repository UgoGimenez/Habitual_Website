Built for the Hardest Conditions

Traditional sanitation fails when infrastructure does. The Mini Module works precisely when and where it is needed most.

Rapid Deployment

Flat-packed and assembles in under 30 minutes. No tools, no machinery, no infrastructure required.

Fully Off-Grid

Zero plumbing. Zero electricity. Operates in remote areas, disaster zones and anywhere water is scarce.

Hygienic & Odorless

Urine-diverting design eliminates odour. Absorbent materials keep the unit clean between uses.

Eco-Friendly

Waste converts to compost over time. No leachate, no blackwater, no environmental contamination.

How It Works

Three steps. No training required.

1

Unpack & Assemble

Ships flat. One person assembles it in under 30 minutes — no tools needed.

2

Use Like a Normal Toilet

The separator keeps solids and liquids apart, eliminating odour without chemicals or flushing.

3

Compost & Reuse

Solid waste becomes compost. Liquid waste can be diluted and used as fertilizer.

Now based in Switzerland, Habitual is bringing this proven solution to European humanitarian organizations and NGOs operating globally.
