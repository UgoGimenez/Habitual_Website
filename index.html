How It Works

Simple enough for anyone. No training required.

1

Unpack & Assemble

Ships flat. One person assembles it in under 30 minutes — no tools, no machinery needed.

2

Use Like a Normal Toilet

The separator keeps solids and liquids apart, eliminating odour without chemicals or flushing.

3

Compost & Reuse

Solid waste becomes compost over time. Liquid waste can be diluted and used as fertilizer.

Proven in the Field

habitual began in Argentina in 2021. By 2024, more than ten units had been sold and remain in active use — built from real-world feedback and hands-on experimentation in the field.

Now based in Switzerland, habitual is bringing this proven solution to European humanitarian organizations and NGOs operating globally.
